About Us

History

The history of the Institute of Engineering and Technology (IET), Lucknow, is a journey that reflects the evolution of technical education in India. Established in 1984, IET Lucknow started its journey as the Engineering College associated with the University of Lucknow. Over the years, it has grown into a prestigious institution in the field of engineering and technology. In 2000, it became an autonomous institution and was subsequently affiliated with Gautam Buddh Technical University (GBTU), now known as Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University (AKTU). The institute has consistently expanded its academic programs and infrastructure, becoming a hub for technical education in the northern region of India. Its commitment to providing high-quality education and fostering research and innovation has allowed it to produce skilled engineers and technologists who have made significant contributions to various industries and sectors. IET Lucknow's history is a testament to its dedication to academic excellence and its mission to nurture the talents of future engineers and technologists.

INFRASTRUCTURE

The infrastructure at the Institute of Engineering and Technology (IET), Lucknow, is a testament to its commitment to providing a top-tier educational experience. The campus is equipped with modern and well-maintained academic facilities, including state-of-the-art classrooms and laboratories that cater to a wide array of engineering and technology disciplines. The institution boasts a well-stocked library, providing students with access to a wealth of knowledge and resources. Comfortable hostel facilities ensure that students have a secure and conducive living environment, and the campus offers recreational amenities such as sports facilities and a cafeteria for a well-rounded student experience. With a focus on IT infrastructure and green initiatives, IET Lucknow ensures that students have access to the latest technology while also being mindful of environmental responsibilities. This comprehensive infrastructure plays a crucial role in fostering a vibrant learning environment and promoting the holistic development of its students.
